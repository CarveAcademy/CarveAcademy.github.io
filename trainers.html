Meet The Mentors


Dr. Mudit Kumar

Periodontist

Implantologist and Laser Specialist

Holding a Bachelor’s degree from Bharati Vidyapeeth Dental College (Pune) and a Master’s degree in Periodontology and Laser Dentistry from JSS Dental College (Mysore), Dr. Mudit Kumar is a seasoned expert in the diagnosis and treatment of all types of gum diseases.

His specialty includes treatment of bleeding gums, smile enhancement, gum de-pigmentation and micro-oral surgeries, which he has performed on a substantial number of patients across multiple regions in the country within a short span of five years.

In addition to being one of the few dental surgeons in Bangalore to specialize in Laser dentistry, Dr. Mudit Kumar had also garnered numerous accolades during his academic life. Some of these include the Dr. V.N. Bhatavadekar Award from the Indian Society of Periodontology (ISP), where he is also a lifetime member, and a Thesis Grant from the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR).



Dr. Bryan Menezes

Dental Surgeon

Chief Dental Surgeon and Implantologist

Having secured his bachelor’s degree from Government Dental College (Bangalore), Dr. Bryan Menezes later specialized in Dental Implantology. He has numerous certifications from the prestigious American Academy of General Dentistry (New Jersey), apart from being a lifetime member of the Indian Dental Association (IDA).

He established his clinical practice - Smile IsleTM Dental Care - in 2012 in Bangalore, starting as a humble single chair setup. Today, Smile IsleTM is among the top 5 preferred dental care centres in Bangalore North for local as well as international patients (as per rankings from vendors), with in-house Hard and Soft Tissue Lasers, a Pediatric Sedation unit, a Cautery unit and several latest radiographic facilities.
